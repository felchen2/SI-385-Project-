Written Explanation:

Some chronic age-related conditions affect older groups, such as hypertension, which has one of the highest median ages, and arthritis. These results align with typical conditions as expected in older age groups. As for conditions that affected younger age groups, asthma presented the youngest median age, which is typical given its prevalence in the younger populations. As for obesity, diabetes, and cancer show medians in the 40s to mid-50s range. This also showed that healthy groups tend to be seen in younger populations, but this group spanned out more to a wider range of age.
